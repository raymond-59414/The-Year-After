Services

The main purpose of our site is to allow anyone to write comfort letters to the pro- files on the website. While doing research on the mournig and grieving process we noticed that one of the biggest factors that keeps a person in pain after loss is the inability to have a conversation with others. With this system we allow people to give some encouragin/comforting words to those in pain, and if they chose to they can become like pen-pals. We believe that this little, simple, human interaction can sometimes make a persons life, or at the very least their day, a bit better. Below you will see a blank space, you can write your letter there. After sending out the mail it will go under revision and then an email will be sent back telling you wheth- er or not the mail has gone through.
