Our Mission

The main purpose of The Year After is to help those going through that resurfacing pain, as well as helping those with trouble moving on. Our goal is to better the general mental health of those who have suffered losses as well as raise community awareness and involvement for these types of issues. Here you are able to send your love, and help, those in your community who have lost a loved one and are going through the pain of loss.
